About Us

PK Tailored Home Care

PK Tailored Home Care services is an Introductory Agency specialising in providing bespoke, Self employed Live in Carers, who are trained and ready to meet your specific needs in the comfort of your own home. This, we believe empowers you to choose carers of your choice as well as manage the care contract the way it best suits you.

We believe that every individual deserves to live with dignity, comfort, and independence, no matter their age or health condition. Our mission is to provide personalised, compassionate, and professional care in the comfort of your own home.

Our home care support services are designed to ease the stress of caring for an elderly or disabled loved one, without requiring you to make the difficult choice of moving them into a care home. When you engage PK Tailored Home Care, you can be rest assured that your loved one’s daily needs will be met with warmth, respect, and compassion.
